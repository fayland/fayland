Category: Diary   Keywords: complaints

If my life had rolled back (like svn revert?), I have decided not to attend the postgraduate entrance examination. hey, it doesn't mean that I fail my exam. Technically speaking, it's too early to say so.
I just feel so tired and want to have a long long rest. I ask my roommate to buy a bus ticket for me and I'll return home within this week.

I'm definitely against this. I devote my time and energy to 2 books which have more than 1000 pages.
The "three" items seem like gambling and I maybe loss during the guess.
In my opionion, it should be more than 20 items account for 110 points.
I'm not sure that I answer the "three big issue" correctly.
Yup, you are right. I am the second object to be reproached because of my laziness.

It's maybe my last examination since my study is nearly close. Woo, freedom.

Some complaints. God bless me.
