Catalyst & Eplanet

Category: Catalyst   Keywords: Catalyst

I'm glad to say it works.

After two days' hard work, I succeed to rewrite my CMS-System Eplanet based on Catalyst. Though my code is awful (Because I don't understand Catalyst or MVC well) and some features are still under construction, it roughly works.

Catalyst is powerful, or sure I say TT & CDBI is great. It saves lots of time. But I still should dig CDBI && TT deep to save more time.

I'll release Eplanet later after I add such features:

  • Textile or Kwid-like formatter, or I should write my own formatter
  • Support the subdir or subcategory
  • different template corresponding diffrent category
  • glossary auto-convert the corresponding URL
  • more ...
God bless me all move smoothly.
