Hey, DONOT waste time anymore

Category: Diary   Keywords: soliloquy

soliloquy

I must confess, I'm always wasting time. Though I do tell myself not to read the useless novels again and again, I just cann't help openning the http://www.cmfu.com/ . God save me. NOW I just say it AGAIN, DO NOT WASTE TIME ANYMORE. And I pray it's the LAST time to hate myself.

I'm 22 years old. NOT a boy any more. I should take the responsibility upon myself, ponder my future and NO time to regret. What's my future? To be frank, I lose my head. What can I do and what do I want? God, I grow up.

Suddenly, time is limited. I'm a senior student, YA. unconsciously.
God bless me. Better late than never.
