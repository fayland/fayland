Day [05.4.24] I got some books for you

Category: Diary   Keywords: books

不是盗版书籍。 暂时只有少数几部，我希望 PerlChina 能开个 FTP。迟点于 hoowa 谈下。

effective_perl.pdf 里的简介是

   Intermediate and Advanced Topics
     a course by Joseph N. Hall
with contributions from Randal L. Schwartz
