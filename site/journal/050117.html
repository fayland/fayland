Goodbye for a while

Category: Diary   Keywords: diary

All exams are finished. I'm on a month winter vacation Now.
I brought a railway ticket to ZhenZhou. It'll start at 6 o'clock today and arrive at 1 o'clock tomorrow afternoon.
I'm not going home, but to see my lovely girl. It's a long time and we have suffered too much.

On the vacation I won't pay lots of time to Net, I take several books from school library: two relatived to XML and one culture book named 《纪德文集》.
I don't think I can read through all of them, 'cause I want to stay with my girl as much as I can. She is all of my life.

2004 is a year out of luck, I won't say anything any more.
May God bless us next year.

Say goodbye & See u all later, have a nice time.
