My first credit card - Young Card

Category: Diary   Keywords: credit card

Today I received a credit card from China Merchants Bank -- Young Card. Yes, it's a credit card only for college students which means no warranty is needed.

The interface of the card is pretty nice. I like it very much.

Yup, I'm looking forward to having a credit card for a long time since I want to buy virtual host at PowWeb last year. That time it bothered me a lot. I asked my sister to borrow a card from her classmate. Ya, so troublesome.

Now, life is much easier.
There is no obstacle any more when I want to buy a book such as "Higher Order Perl" at Amazon.
Another benefit is I can debit some money urgently.
or maybe I can buy a notebook for the benefit of installment plan.

:) I'm a bit excited.
