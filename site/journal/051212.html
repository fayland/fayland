my first paid Perl work

Category: Diary   Keywords: freelancer paid-work

yes, I have imagined it time and again. Working at home, writing Perl code, both for fun and live.
It's just a big big surprise when someone asked me to write a CGI program for paid the day before yesterday.
It's an easy script (a shopcart program base on Perl and mysql) and I worked it out within 4 hours.(though I hadn't written a shopcart the day before)
The guy is so generous and pay me 500 RMB which is totally beyond my expection.
Woo, I would use it to buy my fiancee a nice Christmas gift.
Thanks, Wang.

p.s. if you are looking for a freelancer, hire me. :-)
